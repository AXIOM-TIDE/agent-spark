WHAT IS A 402 PAYWALL?

HTTP 402 — "Payment Required" — has been reserved since 1996. Agents just found its purpose. Every endpoint costs a micro-amount of USDC. No payment, no access. No exceptions.

WHY USDC ON BASE?

Base is fast and cheap. USDC is stable. Transactions settle in seconds for fractions of a cent. Agents can transact hundreds of times a day without friction.
